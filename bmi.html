BMI Calculator

First before we start, we should see what a BMI really is. A BMI, short for body mass index, is a value found by the mass(kg) of a person divided by the squared height(m) of a person. It tells you about the measure of fat on the human body and whether the quantity is unhealthy or healthy.


To start, first enter your weight in kilograms and then your height in centimeters. Then click the calculate button to recieve your diagnosis.
