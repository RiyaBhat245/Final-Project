Exercise

A major part of being healthy includes doing exercise regularly. It is essential that we keep our bodies fit at least. Being healthy reduces one's risk of acquiring chronic diseases later in the future. There are a variety of types of exercises from which people benefit from a combination of. Below are a few examples:
  • Aerobics: Exercises that keep the heart rate high and keep the circulatory system healthy when regulating blood flow throughout the body. Some examples of these exercises are biking, running, swimming, and others.
  • Strength/Resistance: Exercises that allow your muscles to grow and become strong. Examples of these are lifting weights or using a resistance band.
  • Balance: Exercises that can improve your movement on certain types of surfaces. Examples of this would be tai chi or balancing on one leg.
  • Flexibility: Exercises that stretch your body and allow it to stay limber. Examples of this are doing yoga and regularly stretching.
