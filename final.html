Are You Healthy?

Target Audience: Anyone who wants to find more information about their health.


Problem: Many people are not informed about their health.


Why It Will Be Useful: The website will raise awareness and inform people about what they should do concerning their health. It also shows people information about their eating, sleeping, and exercise habits, as well as their BMI (Body Mass Index).
