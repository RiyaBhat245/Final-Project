Food

Another large contribution to your health is having a healthy diet. It is essential to living a sustainable life and provides you with the energy you need to be both physically and mentally active. Some tips to having a balanced diet are:
  • Eat a variety of vegetables, especially those of red, orange, and dark green colors (3 serv.).
  • Eat a variety of fruits (2 serv.).
  • Eat whole-grain breads and cereals (3 - 6 serv.).
  • Reduce or eliminate refined or processed carbohydrates.
  • Drink fat-free or low-fat milk and eat low-fat dairy products.
  • Choose from a variety of low-fat sources of protein (eggs, beans, poultry, seafood, lean meats, unsalted nuts, seeds, and soy products)
  • When using oils, use vegetable oils (like olive or canola oil) instead of solid fats.
  • Reduce daily intake of salt or sodium.

  • Restrict or eliminate sodas and other sugar-added drinks that are high in calories and contain few or no nutrients.
  • If you drink alcoholic beverages, do so in moderation.
